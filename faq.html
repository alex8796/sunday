Q: How can I visit the school?

A: You can call or email us to set up an appointment. Our hours of opperation are from 7:30 am to 6:pm Monday to Friday.

Q: what grade levels do you cater too?

A: Here you will find a nurturing, educational environment for children of ages 6 weeks to 6 years. Classes include infant/ toddler, preschool, and kindergarten.

Q: What is the student to teacher ratio?

A: We understand the importance of individual attention. Our classes are kept small to optimize the learning expirence for your child.

Q: What kind of curriculum do you offer?

A: We recognize that each child has unique talents, learning styles and personalities. Our goal is to maximize creative potential. We offer reading, art, cooking science, trips, math and much more! as well as weekly music and dance classes wit indusrty specialists.

Q: What are the tuition prices?

A: Our tuition is an annual figure. we have comfortable payment plans to meet your budget in either biweekly or monthly payments. for more information refer to our tuition page.
